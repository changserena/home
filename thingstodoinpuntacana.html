Punta Cana

Things to do in Punta Cana

Dune buggy Adventure and Catamaran Cruise

Enjoy riding a 4x4 buggy through the Dominican countryside.Then experiencing a 7 hour tour to a country farm to see how chocolate, cinnamon and coffee is harvested and processed and relax on the white sand on Macao Beach. The Catamaran Excursion allows you to enjoy the coast of Punta Cana Bavaro with no crowds and amazing service on the semi-private excursion. You can snorkel with colorful fishes, swim in turquoise water and relax under the sun.

Parasailing,Sharks and Stingray, Catamaran Cruise and Snorkeling

You can also experience the catamaran cruise but on the wilder side. Experience a beautiful barrier reef surrounded by tropical fish and the fun of an interactive marine show complete with sharks and stingrays. You end the tour by parasailing from the Cortesito Beach above the stunning blue waters and see tropical views through paradise.

Saona Island

A visit to Saona Island, an idyllic speck of sand and palm trees in the blue Caribbean, will include a 10-hour tour from Punta Cana. Board a state-of-the-art speedboat for the thrilling ride out to the island, including a stop at a natural swimming pool filled with starfish. Upon reaching Saona Island, spend the day exploring; stroll along the beach, relax in the white sand and swim in the azure waters. Sip drinks from the open bar and enjoy a buffet lunch of traditional Dominican food.

Chocomuseo

The chocomuseo is ranked the number one museum in Punta Cana. You visit the chocolate factory and learn about the history of cacao and chocolate while witnessing the process of how it is made. You can also make your own chocolate from scratch from the cacao bean and take home samples of delicious homemade chocolate.
